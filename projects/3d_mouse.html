Introducing the 3D Mouse,a cheaper and DIY version of the "Space mouse".

The design that inspired the creation of the 3D Mouse utilizes three joysticks in place of the six hall effect sensors found in my 3D Mouse (thanks to Shiura for the idea). The three joysticks are arranged in a triangular configuration, allowing for the manipulation of 3D models with six degrees of freedom (6DoF). My design is particularly interesting because it allows for a more compact and portable size compared to the original one.

It's the ultimate tool for 3D design professionals. Allows you to intuitively manipulate your 3D models as if you were holding the actual object in your hands. This advanced technology provides unparalleled precision and control, making it easier to create and edit designs, navigate 3D spaces and perform advanced modeling tasks.

With the 3D Mouse, you'll be able to work more efficiently and effectively, saving you time and helping you to produce higher-quality designs. Whether you're an architect, engineer, product designer or digital artist, the 3D Mouse is an essential tool that will help you to bring your ideas to life.

The design is optimized to be 3D printed, even if the base might need some weight to be used properly (we will see, the product is not finished yet).
