You surely have seen a lamp like this online. In my opinion it's cool and also a little bit extreme: I like it!

Designing it is not too difficult: I don't want a copy, just want a Willy shaped lamp that turns on with a strangely familiar motion. A limit switches, pulled by a string attached on the tissue cover should do the trick.

I don't want to mess with high voltage to keep the project safe and easily replicable so a 5 volt led ring will be more than enough for my needs. This will have the plus side of being able to use a 5 volt phone charger as power supply.

The design is optimized to be 3D printed:

  • ∘ fit in common printers (max height 196mm);
  • ∘ NO support needed;
  • ∘ minimum filament usage (ribs are used to make the walls strong without wasting filament).
