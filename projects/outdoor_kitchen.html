This project is a custom request from a client that, in limited space, wanted to create an outdoor kitchen. After understanding all needs of the client I provided 3D model of the project and, after many revisions, the 3D model was completed. To speed up the process a list of all pieces needed was provided to a woodworker and in 2 days the pieces were at destination and ready to assemble. The client assembled himself the puzzle following the provided file and in 3 days the kitchen is ready to be used. He is going to apply a resin to protect the wood and give the top the right heavy look but... not now. Resin need to be applied at the right temperature and humidity and this moment of the year is not the best. See you in March/April for more updates.

I censured the part list but, if you need help, write me, I will be happy to help you.

I'm happy to say that this project does not end here: the customer really wanted a mechanism to lift a beer tap to the top. The kitchen still is an outdoor one so cutting holes in the top to lift it up was not a possibility. The design is almost finished but, due to resin curing ideal temperature and humidity, not yet completed and installed. Updates coming soon...
