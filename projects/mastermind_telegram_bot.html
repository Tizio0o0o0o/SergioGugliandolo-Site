Have you ever had the pleasure of playing the timeless classic, Mastermind? This captivating little game, originating from 1970, continues to enthrall me each time I crack open its diminutive box.

In this technological age, physical board games have become less practical and lack the intellectual allure for younger players. However, fear not, for we may have discovered a modern solution. Are you familiar with Telegram? Well, now you can delve into the world of Mastermind by either perusing the code to create your own version or engaging in a riveting chat-based game with a Telegram bot. Simply strike up a conversation with @mastermind_CS_bot . Rest assured, it should be online and ready for your entertainment. However, on the off chance that it is not, please bear with me. There might be a pesky bug that I am diligently working to squash. Your patience is greatly appreciated. If you're unfamiliar with the rules of Mastermind, simply type "help" in the chat to access a handy guide.
