A one-of-a-kind, handcrafted masterpiece that seamlessly blends industrial design with natural elements. The solid olive wood base anchors the lamp, while the metal round poles arranged in the shape of a tesseract add a modern touch. The bulb at the center of the tesseract casts a warm, inviting light, making it the perfect addition to any room. The rope-like electricity cable adds a rustic charm, completing the overall aesthetic. Each detail of the lamp is thoughtfully crafted, and the slight variations in the metal and wood add to its unique character. Add a touch of sophistication and originality to your space with the Tesseract Lamp.

Every detail is idiosyncratic, every inaccuracy is unique.
