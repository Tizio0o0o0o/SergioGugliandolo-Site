Carrying around a bulky wallet solely for the sake of card dividers is a pet peeve of mine. Why waste precious space when all I really need are my cards and a few handy knick-knacks ?

That's when the brilliant idea struck me: why not create an ingenious solution by enveloping my cards in a stretchy fabric? This revolutionary concept would eliminate any unnecessary extra space, securely housing my cards and bills with the help of rubber linings embedded within the fabric.

But wait, what about those little trinkets that always seem to be in need of a home? That's where the genius idea of incorporating a key holder came into play. Imagine a 3D printed card-shaped marvel, meticulously designed to fit seamlessly into my lifestyle.

Within the confines of this remarkable creation, I can comfortably carry an array of objects, including:

  • ∘ 2 coins;
  • ∘ 1 female USB-A to male USB-C adapter;
  • ∘ 1 USB stick;
  • ∘ 1 clamp to hold together the last 2 items;
  • ∘ 1 micro SD to USB-C adapter;
  • ∘ 4 micro SD;
  • ∘ 1 SIM tool;
  • ∘ 1 big key;
  • ∘ 2 small keys.

All of this neatly tucked away, with a mere 5mm in height (yes, even that chunky key can slide right in!).

The design is meticulously optimized for a flawless 3D printing experience, utilizing a 0.4mm nozzle and a layer height of 0.2mm.

Say goodbye to the hassle of lugging around a cumbersome wallet, and say hello to a stylish and practical solution that will transform the way you carry your essentials. The future is here, and it's all about efficiency, organization, and a touch of innovation.
